Hunter Gender Alliance

We're a not-for-profit charity that's transforming the lives of trans* and gender diverse people by changing policies, advocating, collaborating, and educating.

We've come a long way, and we'd love your help.

Learn more

Advocacy

To promote, empower, and advocate for the health, rights and well-being of the trans* and gender diverse community.

Collaboration

To serve as a forum for and promote communication and collaboration amongst relevant health professionals.

Encouragement

To encourage, promote and share research in applicable fields.

Development

To develop best practices and supportive policies.

Networking

To establish a known network of professional service providers.

Education

We've given a number of educational presentations to medical students (locally and nationally), GPs at the regional conference, Hunter region hospital doctors, nurses, mental health professionals and school counsellors.

We continue to look for opportunities to educate.

HNE-LHD High Value Healthcare Award Finalist 2017

Resources

We've collaborated with HNEkidshealth to develop a resource page for parents and young people who are questioning their gender. This page has information sheets, videos and a resource to print out and take to your GP appointment.

HNE-LHD Innovation Grant Award Winner 2018

Go to HNEkidshealth

HealthPathways

We've developed the Hunter Primary Care Gender Diversity and Transgender HealthPathway to help General Practitioners navigate the health needs of transgender and gender diverse individuals.

HealthPathways is a publicly funded online health information portal for clinicians that provides concise information on how to assess and manage patients.

HNE-LHD High Value Healthcare Award Finalist 2017

Go to HealthPathways

Support Groups

We've set up a much needed local support group for trans* identifying women, as well as a local support group for people who identify as non-binary (there is already a local support group for trans* identifying men).

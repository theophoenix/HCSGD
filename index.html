Advocacy

To promote, empower, and advocate for the health, rights and well-being of the trans* and gender diverse community.

Collaboration

To serve as a forum for and promote communication and collaboration amongst relevant health professionals.

Encouragement

To encourage, promote and share research in applicable fields.

Development

To develop best practices and supportive policies.

Networking

To establish a known network of professional service providers.

Education

We've given a number of educational presentations to University of Newcastle medical students, the Endocrine Society of Australia, ANZPATH, as well as various healthcare providers in the Hunter region.

We continue to look for opportunities to educate.

Finalist of the HNE-LHD High Value Healthcare Award 2017

Resources

We've collaborated with HNEkidshealth to develop a resource page with information sheets and videos for older children, teenagers, and young adults who are questioning their gender. There is also information for parents.

Winner of the HNE-LHD Innovation Award 2018

HealthPathways

We've developed the Hunter Primary Care Gender Diversity and Transgender HealthPathway to help General Practitioners navigate the health needs of transgender and gender diverse individuals.

HealthPathways is a publicly funded online health information portal for clinicians that provides concise information on how to assess and manage patients.

Events

We've been providing assistance with local LGBT+ community events and we'll have a stall set up at the upcoming Newcastle Pride!

Support Groups

We've set up a much needed local support group for trans* identifying women, as well as a local support group for people who identify as non-binary.

There is already a local support group for trans* identifying men.

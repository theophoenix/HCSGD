About Hunter Gender Alliance

In mid 2015, a small special interest group was established with the aim to improve services for trans* and gender diverse individuals in the Hunter region of NSW Australia. From this special interest group, the Hunter Gender Alliance was formed. The current committee includes a variety of healthcare professionals, LGBT+ community members and allies alike that all share a common goal.
